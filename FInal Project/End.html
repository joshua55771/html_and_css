The End

The End is a desolate domention that is very importaint to visit. The End is where you go to beat the game. When you go through the End portal you will see is the most iconic Minecraft boss. the Ender Dragon. When beating the Ender Dragon is not a super difficult boss to beat but it syblizes that your in in the end game. When you have beaten the Ender Dragon you an End Gateway will open. When you go through the End Gateway you will find yourself on the End Islands. It is here where you will find the End City wich contains the most useful item in the game, the Elytra. The Elytra an item that you can where instead of your chestplate that gives you the abliaty to glide. You can then us rockets to propel yourself forward giving you a great way to get around. You can also kill sulkers at the End City. These Sulkers drop their shell in which you can create the Sulker Box. The Shulker Box allowes you to carry more items in your invintory at the same time. This is really usefull for building.
