Why Video Games are Helpful

Creative thinking

Many people belive that video games are a waist of time and that they decrease brain function. In reality gaming is good for you because it enhances cognitive skills like problem-solving, spatial reasoning, and attention, improves emotional and social well-being through stress reduction and community building, and can provide motivational benefits, fostering perseverance and goal-setting. Additionally, certain games can improve hand-to-eye coordination, promote physical activity, and even help delay age-related cognitive decline.
